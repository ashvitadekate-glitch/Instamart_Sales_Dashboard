## 🛒 Swiggy Instamart Sales Analysis
**Power BI dashboard analyzing Swiggy Instamart sales to uncover revenue drivers, outlet performance, and city-tier insights.**

## 🚀 Business Problem

Swiggy Instamart operates across multiple city tiers and outlet types but lacks clear visibility into:

* Which **categories drive the most revenue**
* How **sales vary across city tiers and outlet formats**
* Which **outlet types and sizes perform best**
* Where to focus for **growth and optimization**

### 🎯 Core Business Question:

> How can Swiggy Instamart leverage sales data to identify high-performing segments and optimize revenue growth?

## 🎯 Objective

* Track overall sales performance using key KPIs
* Identify top-performing categories and outlet types
* Analyze sales distribution across city tiers and shop sizes
* Deliver **actionable insights for business decisions**

## 📁 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains transactional and outlet-level information:
** Item Code ,	Category ,	Shop Code ,	Shop , City , Type ,	Shop Size	, Shop Type	, Item Weight	, Sales, 	Rating	,Shop Opening Year

## 📈 Key Insights

* 🛍️ **Top Categories:** Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods each generate ~₹0.18M revenue, making them the highest contributors
* 📍 **City Performance:** Tier 3 cities contribute ~39% of total orders, the highest among all city types
* 🏪 **Outlet Leader:** Supermarket Type 1 generates the highest revenue (~₹7.8L) and order volume
* 📦 **Order Distribution:** Tier-wise orders are relatively balanced, but Tier 3 slightly leads
* 📊 **Outlet Size Impact:** Medium-sized outlets contribute ~42% of total sales, the highest share



## 💡 Business Recommendations

* 📈 **Focus on High-Revenue Categories:** Increase inventory and promotions for top categories
* 🎯 **Expand in Tier 3 Cities:** Strengthen operations in high-performing regions
* 🏪 **Optimize Outlet Strategy:** Invest more in high-performing outlet types
* 📦 **Improve Low-Performing Segments:** Analyze weaker categories and outlet types
* ⭐ **Enhance Customer Experience:** Improve ratings through better service and product quality
